💡 Productivity Tips ▾
1
Silence your phone and put it face-down or in another room before each Pomodoro.
2
Write down distracting thoughts the moment they appear — your brain relaxes once ideas are captured.
3
Define ONE clear task per Pomodoro. Vague goals breed wandering attention.
4
Use breaks to stretch or drink water — not social media. Short dopamine hits make it harder to re-focus.
5
After 4 Pomodoros, review your distraction log to find your personal attention patterns.
6
Background brown noise or lo-fi music can mask environmental distractions without being stimulating.
